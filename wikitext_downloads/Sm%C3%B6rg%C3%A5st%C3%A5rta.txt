{{lang|sv|'''Smörgåstårta'''}} ([[Swedish language|Swedish]] for {{lit|sandwich-[[torte]]}}), or '''sandwich cake''' ({{langx|fi|voileipäkakku}}; {{langx|et|võileivatort}}; {{langx|is|brauðterta}}), is a savoury [[main dish]], not a [[dessert]], of [[Swedish cuisine|Swedish origin]], popular in [[Sweden]], [[Finland]], [[Estonia]], and [[Iceland]], etc. The name is quite literal, with the dish consisting of a [[cake]]-like dish, mainly festive, but composed of layers of bread bound with large amounts of [[sandwich]] filling and garnish, structurally similar to a layered cream cake, but culinarily more in line with [[Toast Skagen]].<ref name="NE">{{Cite web |title=smörgåstårta - Uppslagsverk - NE.se |url=https://www.ne.se/uppslagsverk/encyklopedi/l%C3%A5ng/sm%C3%B6rg%C3%A5st%C3%A5rta |access-date=2024-10-03 |website=www.ne.se}}</ref> When eaten, it is sliced and served like any other cake.

== Preparation and serving ==
A smörgåstårta is typically made up of several layers of white or light [[rye bread]] (albeit dark bread also appear) with creamy fillings in between. The fillings and toppings vary, and may be [[lunch meat]], smoked, graved, or pickled fish (especially [[smoked salmon]]), [[shellfish]] (especially [[shrimp]] and {{ILL|shrimp salad|sv|Räksallad}}), [[caviar]] (or 'caviar cream'), cheeses, [[liver pâté]], cream salads (like [[mimosa salad]]), and sliced vegetables, but [[Egg (food)|egg]], [[mayonnaise]], and [[sourcream]]/[[crème fraîche]] are often the base. Even vegan variants occur. The top garnish often reflects the main ingredients used as a filling.<ref name="NE"/><ref>{{Cite web |last=Maku-toimitus |date=2023-05-22 |title=Voileipäkakku tekee juhlan! Löydä omat suosikkireseptisi herkullisten vaihtoehtojen joukosta |url=https://www.meillakotona.fi/artikkelit/voileipakakku-tekee-juhlan-loyda-omat-suosikkireseptisi |access-date=2024-12-19 |website=Kotona |language=fi}}</ref>

Smörgåstårta is served cold and cut like a dessert cake.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Swedes |first=Neil Shipley's watching the |date=2024-07-29 |title=The Swedish sandwich cake |url=https://watchingtheswedes.com/2024/07/29/the-swedish-sandwich-cake/ |access-date=2024-10-03 |website=Watching the Swedes |language=en}}</ref> It is commonly served at family gatherings such as birthday parties, weddings, or funerals, etc.<ref>{{Cite web |last=halo_altti |date=2023-08-16 |title=Voileipäkakku firar Finlands självständighet |url=https://gruyerestories.com/se/voileipakakku-celebrates-finlands-independence-4/ |access-date=2024-12-19 |website=The Stories of Le Gruyère AOP |language=sv-SE}}</ref>
